Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

Effective date: December 27, 2025 Last updated: December 27, 2025

What information we collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide and improve our services. Types of data we collect include:

  • Account data: name, email address, profile settings and preferences you provide when you register.
  • Usage data: anonymized activity logs, timestamps, feature usage and performance metrics to help improve the product.
  • Device & diagnostics: device type, operating system, browser, and crash reports.
  • Communications: messages you send to support and other optional feedback you provide.

How we use your information

We use data to:

  • Provide and maintain the core service and your account.
  • Personalize your experience and remember preferences.
  • Detect, investigate and prevent activities that may violate our policies or be harmful.
  • Communicate about updates, security notices and support responses.

Legal basis for processing (EEA residents)

If you are in the European Economic Area, we process personal data on these legal grounds:

  • Contract: to provide the service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests: for analytics, fraud detection and product improvement.
  • Consent: where we ask for optional marketing or analytics consent.

Cookies, local storage & trackers

We use small data files such as cookies and local storage to:

  • Keep you signed in and remember preferences.
  • Measure product performance and anonymous usage patterns.
  • Support essential functionality (not optional advertising networks).

You can control cookie settings in your browser. We do not use third‑party advertising cookies.

Sharing and third parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) who are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal authorities when required by law.

Data retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary to provide services and for legitimate business purposes like compliance and fraud prevention. If you close your account we may retain backups for a limited period before permanent deletion.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect your data. This includes encrypted transport (HTTPS), access controls, and routine security reviews. No system is completely secure — if we learn of a material breach we will notify affected users and authorities as required by law.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Export a portable copy of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing in certain circumstances.

To exercise these rights please contact us at pomodify.connect@gmail.com. We will respond within applicable legal timeframes.

Children

Our services are not intended for children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside your residence. When we transfer data we use appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses or rely on adequacy decisions.

Changes to this policy
