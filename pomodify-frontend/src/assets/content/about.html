About Pomodify

Pomodify is built by a small team focused on simple, practical tools to help people work with more focus and less friction. We value privacy, clarity, and performance.

Our mission

Help people build better work habits by providing a minimal, distraction-free timer and session tracking experience.

Principles

  • Privacy-first: minimal data collection and clear controls.
  • Simplicity: quick workflows that avoid unnecessary complexity.
  • Reliability: fast, responsive UI and predictable behavior.
