Support Center

We're here to help. Try the quick suggestions below, or contact support if you need further assistance.

Quick help topics

  • Account access: Reset your password from the sign-in screen or contact support if you can't access your email.
  • Session troubleshooting: Make sure your browser allows audio and timers to run in the background.
  • Data & privacy: Request deletion or export of your account data via Settings or by emailing support.

Contact support

If the topics above don't help, email our support team at hello@pomodify.app including:

  • A short description of the issue
  • Your account email
  • Any relevant screenshots or browser console logs
